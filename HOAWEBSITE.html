Champions Overlook

Milton, GA

Our Neighborhood:

Set on a single tree-lined cul-de-sac street in North Fulton County, Champions Overlook is a community of 25 elegant custom estate homes. The predominantly traditional and European style homes built between 2001-2004 on 1+ acre lots, range in size from 5,000 to over 8,000 square feet, with all homes having 3 or more garages. Champions Overlook is adjacent to beautiful Bell Memorial Park, with baseball fields, walking trails, and playground areas. This neighborhood is close to the GA 400, and minutes from premier dining and shopping in Crabapple, Avalon, and downtown Alpharetta. Champions Overlook is located in the prestigous city of Milton, which has been ranked one of Georgia's safest cities. Close to top-rated schools, first class golf courses, and breathtaking natural parks and surroundings, Champions Overlook is an amazing place to call home.
